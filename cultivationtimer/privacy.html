Privacy Policy

CultivationTimer — Effective February 16, 2026

Introduction

CultivationTimer (“the App”) is developed and operated by Xaenthys (“we,” “us,” or “our”). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is stored, and what choices you have. We built CultivationTimer with privacy as a core principle: the App operates entirely on your device and does not transmit personal data to us or to any third party.

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email address, phone number, or mailing address)
  • Account credentials or login information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising identifiers
  • Analytics, usage statistics, or behavioral tracking data
  • Health or fitness data
  • Photos, contacts, calendar, or other device content

The App contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising frameworks, and no third-party tracking code of any kind.

Information Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device to provide its core functionality. This data never leaves your device.

Data Type Purpose Storage Method
Exercises you create Save your custom timer configurations SwiftData (on-device database)
Practice sets Save grouped sequences of exercises SwiftData (on-device database)
Session history Record completed practice sessions for your statistics SwiftData (on-device database)
Practice goals Track your daily practice targets SwiftData (on-device database)
App preferences Remember your settings (theme, sounds, haptics, volumes) UserDefaults (on-device)

None of this data contains personally identifiable information. Exercises and practice sets are identified internally by random UUIDs, not by any user identity.

In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

The App offers premium features through subscriptions and one-time purchases. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive, process, or store your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID credentials. For information about how Apple handles purchase data, please refer to Apple’s Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following Apple platform service only:

  • Apple App Store / StoreKit: For processing in-app purchases and subscriptions.

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or social media services.

Device Permissions

The App uses the following device capabilities:

  • Background Audio: The App plays sounds (bells, chimes) during timer sessions and continues audio playback when the App is in the background. No audio is recorded. No microphone access is used.
  • Haptic Feedback: The App uses the device’s haptic engine to provide tactile feedback during timer events. This is processed entirely on-device.

The App does not request access to your location, camera, microphone, contacts, calendar, photos, health data, or any other sensitive device capability.

Data Retention and Deletion

All App data is stored locally on your device. You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual exercises, practice sets, or session records within the App.
  • Uninstalling the App, which removes all locally stored data.

Because we do not collect or store your data on any server, there is no data for us to retain or delete on our end.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). Because the App does not collect any personal information from any user, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

International Users

Because all data remains on your device, there is no cross-border transfer of personal data by us.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal data under laws such as the GDPR (EU/EEA), CCPA/CPRA (California), or other applicable privacy regulations. Because the App does not collect personal information and all data resides on your device, these rights are inherently fulfilled:

  • Right to access: Your data is on your device and accessible to you at all times through the App.
  • Right to deletion: You can delete any data at any time within the App or by uninstalling it.
  • Right to portability: Your data is stored on your device and accessible through standard iOS backup mechanisms.
  • Right to opt out of tracking: The App performs no tracking of any kind.

If you have questions about your data or wish to exercise any privacy rights, please contact us using the information below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App’s features or applicable legal requirements. When we make changes, we will update the “Effective” date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
