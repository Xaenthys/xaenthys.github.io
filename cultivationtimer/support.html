Getting Started

What is CultivationTimer?

CultivationTimer is a focused practice timer for meditation, breathwork, and internal cultivation. It supports multi-phase timing (preparation, main practice, and optional extension), saved exercises, and practice sequences.

What's free, and what requires a subscription?

The core timer is completely free, forever — no time limits, no ads. Free accounts can save up to 3 exercises and 1 practice set. Unlimited saves, full practice history, and daily goals require an Unlimited subscription.

How do I create an exercise?

Tap the + button on the Exercises screen. Set a name, configure your timer phases (preparation time, main duration, optional extension), choose sounds, then save. The exercise will appear in your library ready to use.

How do I build a practice set?

Go to Practice Sets and tap +. Add exercises in the order you want to practice them. When you run a practice set, the timer moves through each exercise automatically.

Subscriptions & Purchases

What does Unlimited include?

  • Unlimited saved exercises (free tier: 3)
  • Unlimited practice sets (free tier: 1)
  • Full practice history and statistics
  • Daily practice goals and streaks

What subscription options are available?

Monthly ($2.99), Yearly ($19.99 — best value), and Lifetime ($79.99 one-time purchase). All options unlock the same Unlimited features.

How do I restore a purchase?

Open Settings within the app and tap "Restore Purchases." Your subscription will be verified with the App Store and restored automatically. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone, find CultivationTimer, and tap Cancel Subscription. You'll retain access until the end of the current billing period.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund for any App Store purchase.

Troubleshooting

The timer sounds aren't playing.

Check that your device is not on silent/ring-off. CultivationTimer respects the system mute switch — if the switch is off (orange dot showing), timer sounds won't play. You can also check the volume setting within the app's Settings screen.

The screen turns off during practice.

CultivationTimer keeps the screen awake while the timer is running. If the screen is going dark, check that "Keep Awake During Practice" is enabled in the app's Settings.

My subscription isn't being recognized.

Open Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases." If the issue continues, make sure you're signed into the correct Apple ID and that the subscription is active at reportaproblem.apple.com. If it still doesn't resolve, email us.

I hit the free exercise limit — what are my options?

The free tier supports up to 3 saved exercises. You can delete an existing exercise to make room, or upgrade to Unlimited for unlimited saves. Tap the upgrade prompt in the app to see subscription options.

Privacy & Data

Where is my data stored?

All practice data — sessions, exercises, settings — is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to our servers. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

What happens to my data if I delete the app?

Deleting the app removes all locally stored data. There is currently no backup or sync system (iCloud sync is planned for a future update). Export your data before deleting if you want to keep it.
