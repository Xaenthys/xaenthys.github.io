Getting Started

What is CultivationTimer?

CultivationTimer is a focused practice timer for meditation, breathwork, internal arts, and other contemplative practices. It supports multi-phase timing, saved exercises, practice sets, session history, and daily practice goals.

What’s free, and what requires a subscription?

The core timer is completely free, with no time limits and no ads. The free tier lets you save up to 3 exercises and 1 practice set. Unlimited saved exercises, unlimited practice sets, full session history, and daily goals require an Unlimited subscription.

How do I create an exercise?

Tap the + button on the Exercises screen. Give the exercise a name, configure your timer phases (preparation time, main duration, and optional extension), choose your sounds and haptics, then save. The exercise will appear in your library ready to run.

How do I build a practice set?

Go to Practice Sets and tap +. Add exercises in the order you want to practice them. When you run a practice set, the timer moves through each exercise automatically.

How do I run a timer?

Tap any exercise in your library to open it, then tap Start. For a practice set, tap into the set and tap Start. The timer will guide you through each phase, playing sounds and haptics at each transition.

Subscriptions & Purchases

What does Unlimited include?

  • Unlimited saved exercises (free tier: 3)
  • Unlimited practice sets (free tier: 1)
  • Full session history and statistics
  • Daily practice goals and streaks

What subscription options are available?

Monthly, Yearly, and Lifetime (one-time purchase) options are available. All unlock the same Unlimited features. Current pricing is displayed in the app.

How do I restore a purchase?

Open Settings within the app and tap “Restore Purchases.” Your purchase will be verified with the App Store and restored automatically. Make sure you’re signed into the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. On your iPhone, go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions, find CultivationTimer, and tap Cancel Subscription. You’ll retain Unlimited access until the end of the current billing period.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund for any App Store purchase.

Troubleshooting

Timer sounds aren’t playing.

Check that your device is not on silent. CultivationTimer respects the system mute switch — if the switch is set to silent (orange dot visible), timer sounds won’t play. You can also check the volume setting in the app’s Settings screen.

The screen turns off during practice.

CultivationTimer keeps the screen awake while the timer is running. If the screen is going dark, check that “Keep Awake During Practice” is enabled in the app’s Settings.

My subscription isn’t being recognized.

Open Settings in the app and tap “Restore Purchases.” If the issue continues, confirm you’re signed into the correct Apple ID and that your subscription is still active. If it still doesn’t resolve, contact us directly.

I’ve hit the free exercise limit.

The free tier supports up to 3 saved exercises. You can delete an existing exercise to make room, or upgrade to Unlimited for unlimited saves. Tap the upgrade prompt in the app to see available subscription options.

Privacy & Data

Where is my data stored?

All your data — exercises, practice sets, session history, goals, and settings — is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to our servers or any third party. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

What happens to my data if I delete the app?

Deleting the app removes all locally stored data permanently. There is currently no cloud backup or sync. If you want to preserve your data, do not delete the app. Standard iOS device backups (iCloud or iTunes) will include your app data.

Does the app collect any analytics or track usage?

No. CultivationTimer contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising frameworks, and no third-party tracking of any kind. See our Privacy Policy for the full picture.
